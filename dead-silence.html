Overview

Dead Silence is a first-person, low-light stealth prototype focused on satisfying movement, knife throwing, and strategic decision-making. The core idea is rewarding precision and smart engagement choices rather than pure gunplay.

Core Concept

  • Stealth-first gameplay in low-light environments
  • Throwing knives as the primary skill mechanic
  • Risk/reward: limited knives, retrieval, and missed throws
  • Optional close-range combat when stealth breaks

Gameplay Features

  • First-person movement and interaction loop
  • Knife throw mechanics (aim, release, hit feedback)
  • Class-style attributes (speed / power / balanced)
  • Low-light tone and serious atmosphere
