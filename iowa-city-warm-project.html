Mission Help Iowa City community members stay warm by collecting and distributing warm clothing to people in need.

Impact: We have helped 100+ people with coats, jackets, and winter essentials.

Founded by: Nicholas Wilcox

Donate:Contact Nicholas Wilcox at 712-223-4359

I started the Iowa City Warm Project because I saw a real need in my community. 1 in 4 people in Iowa City do not have their needs met in the winter. I myself, noticed how many people were experiencing homelessness or struggling to get basic essentials. I wanted to help in a simple, direct way: collect coats, jackets, and winter items and deliver them to the people who need them. So far, I’ve organized collected over 100 items and distributed clothing directly to community members and through local connections. My goal is to keep this local, respectful, and focused on real help when temperatures drop. Let's keep Iowa City warm. :)
