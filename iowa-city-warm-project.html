Keeping Iowa City warm — one donation at a time.

The Iowa City Warm Project collects and distributes warm clothing for community members who need it most. Our goal is simple: reduce cold-weather hardship by making coats, jackets, and winter essentials easier to access.

Mission Statement

We exist to support the Iowa City community by providing warm clothing to people facing financial hardship, housing insecurity, or unexpected need—especially during the winter months. We believe warmth is a basic need, and our community is strongest when we look out for each other.

How it works

  • Collect: We accept warm clothing donations (coats, hoodies, gloves, hats, socks, and layers).
  • Sort: Items are organized by size and type so they can be distributed efficiently.
  • Distribute: We share items directly with community members or through local partners and events.

What we need most

  • Winter coats and jackets (all sizes)
  • Gloves, hats, scarves
  • Warm socks and thermals
  • Blankets and hand warmers (new preferred)
