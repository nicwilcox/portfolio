Find thoughtful gifts in minutes.

GiftWise helps you generate personalized gift ideas using recipient profiles (age, interests, dislikes, job, etc.), so you’re not stuck doomscrolling when a birthday or holiday is coming up.

What GiftWise does

Recipient Profiles

Store key details like interests, dislikes, age, and job so gift suggestions match the person.

Track Recipients

Keep a list of people you buy gifts for and reduce repeat purchases across holidays and events.

Suggestion Engine

Generate gift ideas with filters and preferences, then refine quickly until it feels right.

Features

Login & Accounts

Sign up, log in, and manage personal/recipient information securely.

Preference-Based Suggestions

Use interests + dislikes to guide gift ideas and keep recommendations relevant.

Recipient Tracking

Track gifts by person and event so you don’t repeat purchases accidentally.

How it works

Workflow

  1. Create an account and set up your profile.
  2. Add recipients and their preferences.
  3. Generate suggestions and refine based on feedback.
  4. Save what you bought to avoid repeats later.
